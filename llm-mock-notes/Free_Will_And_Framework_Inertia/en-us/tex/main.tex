\title{\textbf{Free Will and Framework Inertia}\\[6pt]
       \large A Semi-Formal Treatment in Textbook Style}
\author{LLM Mock Notes}
\date{April 2026}
\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
We propose \emph{Framework Inertia Theory}, a lightweight formal model that
explains why people often fail to initiate beneficial activities despite
knowing they should. By modelling behavioural states as potential wells and
volitional energy as a depletable resource, we derive that free will achieves
maximal leverage at \emph{decision windows}---brief moments when inertia is
locally minimal---and that \emph{planning} is the principal mechanism for
lowering activation barriers in advance. The treatment is deliberately
semi-formal: definitions and theorems provide structure, while discussions and
examples supply intuition.
\end{abstract}
