\begin{abstract}
This draft upgrades the earlier short note into a research-backed textbook
prototype. The structure now combines a behavioural framework with three new
layers: consciousness biology, emergence, and advanced dynamical systems.
The goal is not to claim final rigor, but to create a manuscript architecture
that can support systematic teaching without confusing strong empirical work,
mechanism-building theory, and speculative frontier hypotheses. The central
claim remains unchanged: free will is most effective when it is deployed at
low-inertia transition points and reinforced by pre-committed structure.
\end{abstract}
