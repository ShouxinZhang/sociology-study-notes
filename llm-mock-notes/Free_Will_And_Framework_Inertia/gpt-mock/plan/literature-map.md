# Literature Map For Research Expansion

## Purpose

This document classifies the sources needed for the next planning cycle of
`Free_Will_And_Framework_Inertia`. The new scope adds:

1. frontier consciousness biology and neuroscience,
2. emergence and framework formation,
3. advanced dynamical systems,
4. chapter-level recommended reading appendices built from classic textbooks
   and major reviews.

Evidence tags:

- `strong`: suitable for the empirical core of the manuscript
- `medium`: suitable for interpretation, mechanism building, or mathematical framing
- `speculative`: useful only in frontier or hypothesis sections, not as core evidence

## Business Outcome

This map prevents the next version of the manuscript from becoming an
undisciplined literature dump. It gives each source a role, an evidence grade,
and a chapter destination, while also ensuring that classic textbooks become a
deliberate educational layer through chapter-level recommended reading
appendices.
